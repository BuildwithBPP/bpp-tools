Understanding Your Metrics

Not all metrics are equal. Some tell you how many people saw your content. Others tell you if anyone cared.

👁
Reach
Number of unique people who saw your post. Like foot traffic past a storefront. Doesn't mean they watched or engaged.
Awareness. Distribution, not impact.
▶
Views
Times your content was viewed or played. One person can view multiple times. High view-to-reach ratio = sticky content.
Attention. Did people actually watch?
📊
Impressions
Every time your post appears on a screen, including repeats. Useful for understanding algorithm distribution.
Distribution. Less actionable than reach.
❤️
Likes
Lightest engagement. Someone tapped the heart. Low effort, low signal. Lots of likes but no shares/saves = appreciated but not impactful.
Soft signal. Doesn't drive growth.
💬
Comments
Someone stopped and typed something. Algorithm weights this heavily. Posts with comments get shown to more people.
Conversation. Algorithm loves this.
🔄
Shares
Strongest growth signal. Someone sent your content to someone else. This reaches people who don't follow you. Your organic reach multiplier.
Growth. How you reach new audiences.
🔖
Saves
Strongest intent signal. Someone bookmarked your post. Instagram's algorithm weights saves heavily. Content gets pushed longer.
Intent. Highest algorithm weight on IG.
➕
Follows
Someone saw your content and followed your account. The ultimate single-post win. Financial Reels drive the most follows for BPP.
Conversion. Viewer became a follower.
⏱
Watch Time
How long people watched before scrolling. Most important video metric. High views + low watch time = people bounced. Not in IG CSV. Check app or connect API.
Retention. Did your hook hold attention?
⏭
Skip Rate
% who scrolled past without watching. 30% skip = 70% watched. Your hook quality score. Only in IG app, not CSV.
Hook quality. First 1-2 seconds.
🖱
Clicks
Someone tapped to learn more, visit profile, or click a link. Active interest beyond passive viewing.
Action. Someone wanted more info.
📐
Engagement Rate
Total engagements / reach as %. Industry avg 1-3%. BPP Reels avg 6.1%, carousels avg 20.4%.
Quality. Did content resonate?
Which Metrics Matter Most?

Growing audience

Views, Follows, Shares. Reels format. Financial tips as topic.

Building trust

Saves, Engagement Rate. Carousels format. Frameworks and checklists.

Spreading through networks

Shares. Community/identity content gets shared 3-5x more.

Converting to action

Clicks, Watch Time. Photos drive FB clicks. 10-12s watch time = high engagement.

Platform Strategy

Instagram

Primary platform. 4–6x/week. Reels drive 3.5x more reach than other formats. Post IG first, cross-post to TikTok + Facebook same day.

LinkedIn

Most untapped — 9.1% CTR when active. 2x/week Tue + Thu Statics. Write for established operators and service-based business owners.

Cross-post only. Same Reels as Instagram. No original production for these platforms. Favorites on TikTok = strongest signal.
