Tips for Using Skills
You don't need to memorize triggers. Just describe what you need and the right skill activates.
Combine skills by asking naturally: "Write a cold email to Tampa CDC using our brand voice."
Skills read BPP context automatically -- they know your ICP, packages, brand voice, and team.
If the wrong skill triggers, say "use the [name] skill for this instead."
All workspace skills sync via OneDrive. Any machine that opens the BPP Workspace gets them.
