Q2 Outlook and Action Plan
What needs to happen next
Where We Are
Q1 cash revenue was $7,279 against a $15,000 target (48%). HubSpot shows $9,050 in closed deals, with a $1,771 collection gap. Net margin hit 23%, within the 20-25% target, but only because absolute numbers are low. As revenue scales, margin needs to hold.

Lost deals ($11,751) exceeded won deals ($9,050). The team closed at a 32% win rate, which is competitive, but the volume of lost value points to qualification issues the new ICP framework should fix.

Q1 was the pre-ICP period. Pricing was inconsistent. Packages weren't standardized. The team was still building the operating system. That context matters.
Where We're Going
The ICP and packages are finalized. Three tiers. Clear qualification criteria. Standardized delivery playbook. This didn't exist in Q1.

The pipeline is real. $21,697 across 7 deals. Lois Marketing ($4,500) and Twanda Bradley ($5,197) alone are $9,697. If both close, Q2 outpaces Q1 on two deals.

The leverage point is delivery. Lois Marketing is the first Operator System engagement. Nail it. Get the testimonial. That case study becomes the sales tool for the next 3 deals at $4,500 each. That's the path from $7K quarters to $15K+ quarters.
5 Things to Fix in Q2
1. Software costs eating 57% of expenses. Audit every subscription. Microsoft 365 renewal ($600) inflated March, but the baseline is still too high. Target: under 40%.

2. Close the HubSpot-to-QB gap ($1,771). Deals marked closed but cash not collected. Enforce the 7-day late payment clause. Make collection a standing weekly task.

3. Recategorize QB income by package tier. Current categories (Consulting/Marketing/Technology) don't map to the new package structure. Without this, you can't track revenue per tier.

4. Start tracking time per deliverable. Clockify is in the backlog. Lois Marketing is the test case. Track hours per milestone so you know whether $4,500 is priced right.

5. Connect content to pipeline. 48K views and $277 CPA, but no way to trace which posts generate leads. Set up UTM links and GA goals, or upgrade Metricool to Advanced.
